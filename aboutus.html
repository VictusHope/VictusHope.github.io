Justin Sanderson - CEO

Justin Sanderson was born under mysterious circumstances in 1993 in the city of Jacksonville, Florida. As an entrepreneur and artist from a young age, he quickly earned a cult following in his hometown and used it to positively benefit the lacking community.

Justin worked primarily to direct and provide entertainment to large crowds of people because of his profound interest in Human psychology and creating a positive space for people. Due to his professional and creative success, his family selected him as the inheritor of the Victus Technologies company and fortune.

Besides his astounding devotion to the reformation and success of Victus Technologies, Justin's hobbies include socializing, going out at night, and the arts and sciences.

Mission Statement

At Victus Technologies, our companies' primary motivation resides in providing cutting-edge digital development and design services to clients from all over the globe. We aim to provide our prospective clients with a comprehensive and creative resource that will allow them to easily manifest the dream that they have for their company, start-up, personal business, or beyond.

With an array of professional designers and an expansive network, our company strives to provide a professional, thorough, and straight-forward approach to making your creative and professional dream come alive. Utilizing resources acquired from nearly eighty years of technological development, our designers are able to provide websites and applications that will deliver the quality and comfort frequently found in successful, professional ventures.

State-of-the-art graphic design coupled with inspiring talent of traditional artists gives our clients endless opportunity to fully create the vision that drives them.
