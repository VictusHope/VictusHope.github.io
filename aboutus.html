VictusTech History

Founder

As Victus Technologies has been operating under the radar for over eighty years, we see it as our duty to clarify and correct some of the misconceptions about our company and provide an accurate summary of our history. Victus Technologies was founded on December 13th, 1941 as an emergency coalition to combat the technological advances being made by Germany and the Axis powers. Throughout the war, Victus Technologies assisted in the development of numerous inventions, devices, and technologies that would be invaluable in the war. Our company contributed heavily, both before and after the war, to the invention of the pressurized cabin, jet engine propulsion, and nuclear technology. In the following decades, Victus Technologies would continue to innovate heavily through the United State's government's funding and promise of both secrecy and privacy. Due to the influence of the Cold War era, the company was asked to and succeeded in pushing the boundaries in the fields of computers, space-flight, and genetic science. Before the end of the company's government contract in 2018, Victus Technologies had successfully developed and implemented several black project technologies that were thought to be impossible for most of the twentieth century. With our non-disclosure and service contracts having expired with the United States government, we enter into a new era of Victus Technologies. We will continue to contribute to our hidden history by expanding our historical document collection in our public archives.
